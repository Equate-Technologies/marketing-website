FAQ

Pricing questions.

What counts as a "site"?
One physical location with at least one Equate node deployed. Multiple nodes at the same address count as one site.
Is monthly billing available?
Yes, at a 20% premium over the annual rate. Annual plans are prepaid and non-refundable after 30 days.
What's locked in for early access?
Early access members lock in the discounted rate above for the lifetime of their subscription — even as we raise prices for new customers.
Do I pay for cellular data separately?
Yes. SIM costs are separate. We recommend a 1–2 GB/month data-only plan per site ($5–20/month depending on carrier).
