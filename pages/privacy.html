Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Equate Technologies, Inc. ("Equate", "we", "us") collects information you provide directly to us, such as your name and email address when you join our waitlist or create an account. We also collect usage data and telemetry from the Equate agent software installed on your devices, including heartbeat signals, session metadata, and connection events.

We do not collect the content of OOB sessions, terminal input/output, or any data transmitted over the out-of-band management path.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve the Equate platform; send you technical notices and support messages; respond to your comments and questions; and send you marketing communications, from which you may opt out at any time.

Session metadata (timestamps, device identifiers, connection duration) is stored to power audit logs and usage analytics. This data is never sold to third parties.

3. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Audit log data is retained according to your plan tier (30 days for Starter, 1 year for Pro, custom for Enterprise). You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting privacy@equate.io.

4. Data Security

All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.3. Data at rest in our AWS infrastructure is encrypted using AES-256. We employ industry-standard security practices and undergo regular third-party security reviews. See our Security page for more detail.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating the platform (AWS, Stripe, etc.) under strict data processing agreements. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of Equate and its users.

6. Cookies

We use strictly necessary cookies to maintain your authenticated session. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser, but this will prevent you from accessing authenticated areas of the platform.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data; object to or restrict our processing of your data; and receive a copy of your data in a portable format. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@equate.io.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated effective date and, where appropriate, by sending an email notification.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at privacy@equate.io or via our contact page.
