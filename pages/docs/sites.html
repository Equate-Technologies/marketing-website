Managing sites

Learn how to add, configure, and organize your distributed sites.

Adding a new site

To add a new site, navigate to Settings → Sites → Add Site. You'll receive a unique site token to use during agent authentication.

Site grouping

Organize sites into logical groups (by region, function, or team) for easier management and access control.
