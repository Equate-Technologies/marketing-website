Platform

Everything you need
when things go wrong.

Built for IT teams managing infrastructure across multiple sites — not just one data center.

Hardware-Agnostic
Works with your existing console servers and cellular routers. No proprietary hardware required — bring your own device and install our agent.
AWS-Native Infrastructure
All traffic routes through AWS, inheriting enterprise-grade reliability, global regions, and security certifications your compliance team already trusts.
Role-Based Access
Granular permissions per site and device. Only the right engineers access the right infrastructure. Full SSO support included.
Immutable Audit Logs
Every OOB session is logged, timestamped, and tamper-proof. Built for SOC 2, ISO 27001, and any compliance framework that demands accountability.
Instant Failover Alerts
The moment a primary link drops, Equate detects it, establishes the OOB path, and notifies your team — before your monitoring tools even catch it.
Session Analytics
Track OOB usage, failover frequency, and mean time to resolution across your entire fleet. Identify problem sites before they become incidents.
How it works

Up and running in under an hour.

No hardware to manufacture. No complex VPN configurations. Just a lightweight agent and a supported cellular device.

1
Deploy the agent
Install our lightweight agent on a supported device at each site. Compatible with most off-the-shelf cellular routers.
2
Register your sites
Each device checks in with Equate over AWS IoT Core. Sites appear in your dashboard within minutes.
3
Set access policies
Define which engineers can access which sites. Configure alert thresholds and notification channels.
4
Connect when it counts
When a primary link fails, log into Equate from anywhere and get a secure terminal session to your devices in seconds.
